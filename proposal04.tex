\subsection*{Data}

The dataset we are using is at: \url{https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~jmcauley/datasets/amazon_v2/}. Since it is a large dataset, we selected a category with sufficient data relevant to our project. We chose the Electronics category, which contains 22 million reviews and ratings for over 787k products. The compressed file is approximately 3GB. We retain five fields for analysis: \texttt{overall} (rating 1--5), \texttt{verified}, \texttt{asin} (product ID), \texttt{reviewText}, and \texttt{summary}. We used the complete dataset rather than the 5-core subset in order to retain full coverage before applying our own filtering criteria.

Several data cleaning steps will be performed. We will remove reviews with fewer than 20 words to exclude low-content entries, which reduces the dataset from 22 million to approximately 12.3 million reviews. We will remove products with fewer than 200 reviews to ensure each product has sufficient data for meaningful analysis. We will then randomly cap at 500 reviews per product (seed=42) to prevent popular products from dominating the dataset. After cleaning, we expect approximately 3.94 million reviews across 11,000 products.
